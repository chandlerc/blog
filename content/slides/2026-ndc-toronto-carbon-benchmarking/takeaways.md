## Takeaways

- Always measure if you care about performance
  - Telemetry to measure the actual system in the real world
  - Benchmakrs to measure _changes_ and drive _improvements_
- Invest in your benchmarks!
  - Make sure they measure what you _actually_ care about
  - Make sure they measure it in a consistent (even if noisy) way
  - Run them in ways that give a good model of expected behavior
  - Profile them to make sure they're measuring what you think

---

## Takeaways

- Start with data structures, always
  - Most of Carbon's performance comes from those
  - And the optimizations rely on them
- Keep caring about performance and efficiency!!!
